# 📑 View my account information

1. Log in at [https://puf.visoftware.tech](https://puf.visoftware.tech) with your Discord account that is linked to the VIS Discord.
2. Once you're on the dashboard, navigate to 'My Accounts' using the top navigation bar.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (4).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Once on this page, you will be able to see all your accounts, including their usernames, tokens (passwords), rank, team, activity status, and quick access to manage the account (such as changing the skin, updating the password, etc.).

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (1) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Para ver su token, ponga el ratón encima de la palabra "OCULTO"</p></figcaption></figure>



{% hint style="danger" %}
REMEMBER! Ensure that the password does not have any spaces or other characters before contacting VI Software support if you are unable to log in.
{% endhint %}
