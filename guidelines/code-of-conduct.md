# 📔 Code of Conduct

Our open-source community is dedicated to providing a safe, inclusive, and welcoming environment for all contributors. To foster a positive and collaborative space, we have established this Code of Conduct that applies to all community members, contributors, and maintainers.

1. **Be Inclusive and Respectful**:\
   Accept diversity and treat everyone with respect and kindness.\
   Embrace different perspectives and experiences.\
   Avoid offensive comments, discriminatory language, and personal attacks.
2. **Maintain a Welcoming Environment**:\
   Be considerate and supportive of others.\
   Be mindful of your words and actions, as they may affect others in the community.\
   Welcome newcomers and help them feel included.
3. **Practice Empathy**:\
   Understand different opinions and experiences.\
   Listen actively and consider the feelings of others.\
   Be open to learning from each other.
4. **Respect Privacy**:\
   Avoid sharing personal information about others without their explicit consent.\
   Respect individuals' choices regarding privacy and disclosure.
5. **Handle Disagreements Constructively**:\
   Disagreements are a natural part of any community. Address them respectfully and professionally.\
   Focus on the issue at hand and avoid personal attacks.\
   If a disagreement arises, seek mediation or involve community leaders.
6. **Report Violations**:\
   If you witness or experience any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, report it to the community leaders.\
   Complaints will be treated confidentially and thoroughly investigated.
7. **Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior**:\
   Unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated.\
   Consequences for violations may include warnings, temporary or permanent exclusions, or other actions deemed appropriate by the community.
8. **Community Leadership**:\
   Community leaders are responsible for enforcing the Code of Conduct.\
   Leaders have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
9. **Scope**:\
   This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including but not limited to GitHub repositories, forums, discussions, and events.
10. **Our Promise**:\
    In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we commit to upholding and enforcing this Code of Conduct consistently and fairly.\
    Adherence to this Code of Conduct helps create a positive and inclusive environment where all contributors can collaborate and thrive. We appreciate your commitment to making our community welcoming to all.
