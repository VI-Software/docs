# 📔 Privacy Policy

------------------------------

1. **Information Collected and Used**

1.1. **Account Data:**\
When registering for VIS' Yggdrasil Auth, we collect and store the necessary information for account creation, which may include your username, email address, and password.

We use an authentication system similar to Yggdrasil to ensure account security and prevent unauthorized access. This involves the generation and storage of access tokens associated with user accounts.

3.2. **Legal Compliance:**\

4. **Security**

4.1. **Data Protection:**\
